Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What we collect

We collect your email address and name when you register or sign in with Google. We also store usage data (which videos you watched and your progress) to provide the service.

2. How we use it

We use your data solely to operate babook — to track your learning progress, send you account-related emails, and provide customer support. We do not sell your data.

3. Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in, and a preference cookie to remember your cookie consent. We use Plausible Analytics for anonymous site statistics — no personal data is collected by analytics.

4. Data retention

Your account data is kept as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting us at privacy@babook.co.il.

5. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@babook.co.il.
