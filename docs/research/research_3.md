# Research Phase 3 — The Real Business Model

**babook.co.il is a marketing engine to sell Avi as a premium AI lecturer**  
**Date:** May 20, 2026  
**Status:** Strategic pivot. This reframes everything.

---

## Executive Summary

The platform is not a SaaS product. It's a credibility and lead-generation system. The real revenue is corporate training engagements at ₪15-50K per gig. The platform's job is to make Avi's phone ring.

---

## 1. The Real Business Model

| Layer | Purpose | Revenue |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **babook.co.il** (platform) | Marketing & credibility engine | ₪0 (or minimal/break-even) |
| **Avi the lecturer** (the product) | Corporate training, workshops, keynotes | ₪15-50K per engagement |

The platform doesn't need to be profitable on its own. It needs to:
1. Build Avi's authority as THE AI expert in Israel
2. Generate inbound leads from HR directors and engineering managers
3. Create social proof that closes deals

---

## 2. Why This Changes Everything

### What we THOUGHT the business was:
- SaaS platform with subscriptions
- Token resale margins
- Marketplace commissions
- Scale to 10K+ users for revenue

### What the business ACTUALLY is:
- Authority platform (free content builds brand)
- Audience capture (newsletter + community = lead pool)
- Social proof machine (certificates issued, testimonials, logos)
- Corporate sales funnel (platform → trust → booking → ₪₪₪)

### Implications:

| Previous Assumption | New Reality |
|--------------------|-------------|
| Token resale is primary revenue | Token resale is a nice hook, not the business |
| Need 10K users for ₪1M revenue | Need 20 corporate gigs at ₪25K = ₪500K |
| Marketplace must be profitable | Marketplace positions Avi as curator/expert |
| Community needs monetization | Community is free marketing fuel |
| Global scale needed | Israel-only is correct (corporate training is local) |
| Platform is the product | Platform is the funnel. Avi is the product |
| Billing/payments are P0 | Billing is P3 at best. Authority is P0 |

### How users flow through:

1. **Discovery:** Dev googles "AI training Israel" or sees LinkedIn post → finds babook.co.il
2. **Value:** Takes free course, joins community, reads newsletter → trusts Avi
3. **Social proof:** Sees certificate counter, testimonials, company logos → believes Avi delivers
4. **Internal champion:** Shows babook to their manager → "We should bring this guy in"
5. **Manager acts:** Clicks "Corporate Training" → contacts Avi → books engagement
6. **Delivery:** Avi delivers ₪20-50K workshop → gets testimonial → feeds back into platform

## 6. Israel-Specific Strategy

### Why Israel-Only is Correct

1. **Corporate training is local.** Companies want someone who shows up, speaks Hebrew, understands culture.
2. **Network effects are local.** One happy HR director tells 5 others at similar companies.
3. **Market is concentrated.** 200-500 companies with AI training budgets in Israel (high-tech, defense, finance).
4. **Face-to-face closes deals.** You can meet decision-makers at conferences, meetups, intros.
5. **Hebrew content is rare.** English AI content is infinite. Hebrew practical AI content barely exists.

### Target Companies (Who Pays ₪20-50K for Training)

| Segment | Examples | Budget | Decision Maker |
|---------|----------|--------|----------------|
| Big tech (Israel R&D) | Intel, Google, Microsoft, Apple | ₪50-100K | Engineering VP / L&D director |
| Defense/national | Rafael, Elbit, IAI, Unit 8200 alumni | ₪30-80K | Training officer / CTO |
| Finance | Bank Leumi, Hapoalim, insurance cos | ₪30-50K | Chief Data Officer / CTO |
| Startups (Series B+) | 100+ funded startups | ₪15-30K | CTO / VP Engineering |
| Consulting/services | Deloitte, Accenture, local firms | ₪20-40K | Partner / Practice lead |

### Revenue Model (Conservative)

| Year | Gigs/Month | Avg Price | Annual Revenue |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Year 1 (building) | 1-2 | ₪20K | ₪240-480K |
| Year 2 (established) | 3-4 | ₪30K | ₪1.1-1.4M |
| Year 3 (premium) | 4-5 | ₪40K | ₪1.9-2.4M |

20 gigs per year at ₪25K average = **₪500K**. That's the realistic Year 1 target.

## 7. Content Strategy (What to Teach for Free)

### Courses That Position You (Not Compete with Coursera)

| Course | Why It Works as Marketing |
|--------|--------------------------|
| "AI for Engineering Managers" | Decision-makers take it → book you for team |
| "GitHub Copilot for Your Team" | Practical, trendy, shows you're current |
| "Building AI Agents (Practical)" | Hot topic, demonstrates deep expertise |
| "From Zero to AI: Engineer's Path" | Broad appeal, entry point to funnel |
| "MCP Servers: Build Custom AI Tools" | Cutting edge, positions as innovator |

### Content that sells lectures (not courses):

- **Short (2-4 hours)** — gives a taste, leaves them wanting more
- **Practical** — managers see team applying it immediately
- **Current** — latest tools (Copilot, Claude, MCP), not textbook ML
- **Opinionated** — "Here's what actually works" (authority positioning)
- **Hebrew** — zero competition in this space

- Token resale margins
- Marketplace GMV
- DAU/MAU ratios

These are SaaS metrics. You're not building a SaaS. You're building a personal brand engine.
